Nested Lists

A nested list is a list created in another list e.g:

Among the wide variety of delicacies sold at Akinwale Restaurant are:

  • Ice creams
  • Pastries
    • Buns
    • Cakes
    • Croissants
    • Pan-cakes
  • Tarts
  • Yoghurt
